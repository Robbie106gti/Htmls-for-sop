A new species of wood that we are promoting.

It is technically the Eucalyptus hardwood. It is Cherry toned in color, and is comparative to Oak in strength and density.

General information:

  • It is from Brazil.
  • It is a hardwood.
  • It is a farmed tree.
  • It takes 25 years to mature.
  • It is a mahogany replica.

Nickels options:

  • Not available with enviro core.
  • Price the same as cherry. (Cherry Carmel VGM)
  • Available only as an Alpha VGM and a Carmel VGM door.
  • It is available in Bronze, Chocolate, Cinnamon, Cognac, Copper, Java and Paprika plus custom upon request.
  • No FE’s are available, only AG’s.
  • Available mouldings are: HMR-SQ, HM-SQ, RM, HMR-SQ, PKM-SQ, W4, AM-SQ, all valances with an SQ edge detail, FSM1-C and FSM1R-C.
  • Sample blocks will be 4” x 4” x 3/16” thick veneer.
