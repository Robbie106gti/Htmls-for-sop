Appliance Frames:

Don’t finish the back of these frames unless otherwise specified.

MSC-ODF, MSC-OFS, MSC-OFF2, MSC-OFF4, MSC-OFF4 and MSC-OFF5 Frames:

Finish the back of these frames unless otherwise specified.

How to attach Frames:

When it says “Ship Loose”, we do nothing.
When it says “Attach in shop”, we will pin (from the inside when possible) and glue it into position. We will biscuit it into place if there are no pinning options.
When it says “Dry Fit”, we will prepare the frame and cabinet with biscuits to be glued on site. The frame will be taped to the cabinet in our shop with painters tape.
