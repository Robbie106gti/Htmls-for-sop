SOSR-WD (Wood slide out storage racks)
  • Material = Maple or Baltic Birch Plywood. Note that the standard is Maple and that the Baltic Birch Plywood will require a special note. Baltic Birch Plywood isn’t in the catalog or WebQuoin. If the SOSR says nothing regarding the material, it is to be Maple.
  • Th= 6mm for Maple and 9mm for Baltic Birch Plywood
  • Finish = Natural-Satin
  • Set shelves are doubled in thickness (Maple = 32mm, Plywood = 30mm).
  • Use the DFS system to attach to the door so that the door has adjustability.
  • The SOSR is always centered on the door, in height and width. The SOSR H = the door height minus 61mm.
  • Don’t completely attach the backing until the SOSR has been installed. This is to avoid any bowing cabinets that may affect the installation of the SOSR.
SOSR-LD
  • When the door square footage is < 4 square feet we will use the SOSR LD spec’s.
  • Hardware = Accuride softclosing slides from Richelieu
  • W=opening – 26 mm.

Note: we tried the KV softclosing slides from Butler Marketing which didn't work well.

Roles of the different teams for an SOSR:
E

: Note HD or LD and the cabinet it is going into. Order HD slides if required.

D:

Take the doors to the P2P machine and have them drilled for the DFS. Note HD or LD.

B:

Cut parts. Machine the SOSR front and back for the DFS and adjustable shelves. Machine the doors for the DFS.

PA:

Take the SOSR fronts to the P2P machine and have them drilled for the DFS. Take the SOSR fronts and backs to the P2P machine and have them drilled for adj. shelves when applicable. Assemble.

F:

Finish it.

FA:

Install. Note to completely attach the backing after the installation of the SOSR.
