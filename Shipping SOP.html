Shipping: SOP

If an order doesn’t ship out on it’s specified day, the scheduler will notify the appropriate Order desk person who will let the dealer know the situation. Order desk is not to take the word of any Team except the actual shipper as to whether an item is being shipped. If you have to, check back at the end of the day.

If you need to ship a very small item to a dealer and the dealer doesn’t want to wait for their next delivery of cabinets, that item can be sent in an envelope in the mail. Here’s our system:

  • The item must be entered into quoin along with a note saying that Reception is mailing it.

  • Give Reception the item and the paperwork.

If an item can’t be shipped on a pallet and there is a chance of it being damaged, we will make a wood box for it (e.g. A pack of bumpers being sent as an individual item, doesn’t need to have a wood box. A sample door not being sent on a pallet needs a wood box.).

Shipping will notify PA to make an appropriately sized wooden box.

When a dealer is getting a small item that's going on a truck that is already going there. Do we charge?

The individual order shipping charge does not change even when 1 such item is ordered and shipped. This most definitely results in a net shipping loss to Nickels. So the option is to then charge appropriately for that 1 item which will cost the dealer between $200.00 and $500.00. This gets us into a minimum shipping charge scenario and we've been there before with greater annoyances and headaches.

The individual shipping charge is designed to somewhat offset aforementioned losses while controlling the dealers shipping costs.
