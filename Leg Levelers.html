Standards

· 4 5/8” minimum cabinet depth for leg levelers to fit with a standard kick. If the cabinet depth is less than this, we won’t attach hubs and the installer will have to build it on site, unless otherwise specified.

Staple amounts

· 4 staples per hub.

Leg amounts

  • All cabinets with leg levelers have 2 legs at the front and back of the cabinet. If the cabinet is > 39”, it will receive 3 legs at the front and back of the cabinet.

  • BS cabinets > 33” get a leg in front center, with 4 staples.

  • All blind corner cabinets with leg levelers have 3 legs across the front (to give extra strength for the baseboard butting into it) and 2 across the back. If the cabinet is > 39” wide, there are to be 3 legs across the back also.

  • All Tall cabinets with leg levelers have removable support strips (3”W x 1”H x 13” long –doubled up ½” K3 material) attached to the bottom of the cabinet. The strips are to be attached with 1 ½” pins. The strips protect the gables from damage while in transport. See sketch for the positioning of the support strips.
