DS-Tandembox Drawer Box change Spec's
What we are currently using:

It is called Tandembox plus.
The white (our name is DS-WTBM) and stainless (our name is DS-STBM) will be discontinued at the end of this year, errrrrr?????
The grey (our name is DS-GTBM) will still be available… for now.

A potential new option and what we will probably use:

It is called Tandembox Intivo (Not In Vitro).
Available with White, Black and Stainless sides.
The M sides don’t have box side ability.
The L sides must be used when box sides are wanted.
Available with metal or glass boxsides.
No gallery rails.

A potential new option and what we will probably not use:

It is called Tandembox Antaro (Not Pantera).
Available with White and Stainless sides.
Not available with boxsides.
The gallery rails are rectangular.
The drawer sides are all M sides and are the exact same as used in the Intivo line.
