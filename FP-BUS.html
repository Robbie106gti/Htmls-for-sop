Construction standards:

Made out of 5/8” thick material.
They have ribs following the same rules as our columns.

Mitered corners:

If it is ≤ 42” high, it has mitered corners. These pieces will have to be cut 3mm wider to allow for trimming room.
If it is ≤ than 24” wide, the angle will be cut by the PA team.

If it is > than 24” wide, the angle will be cut by the B team.

Butt joint corners:

If it is > 42” high, it has butt joints and is veneered on the sides.

Standard

½” inset at the bottom for scribing.

If it is for the finished top and bottom application:

Top and bottom are flush and veneered.
