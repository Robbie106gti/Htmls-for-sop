Situation:

The customer wants to be able to push a door or drawer front and have it pop out a little bit so the can grab its edge and open it. This is usually because there is no handle on the door or drawer front.

Customer notes:
  • For best use practices, always push in the middle of the drawer box.
  • The installer must first adjust the drawer front to appropriate spacing/level and then add 2 screws through the drawer box into the drawer front and 2 at the bottom of the drawer box through the predrilled holes in the slide hardware into the drawer front. These screws are required for extra support because of the stress applied from using the tip on system.

Links to detailed specs. and info. for the Tandembox drawer application:

Steps:
  • E: Order hardware and optimize parts.
  • B: Cut the optimized parts.
  • DA: After identifying the bottoms that require Tip On hardware, bring them to the P2P machine for machining.
  • B: Machine the bottoms using the parametric program called Tip On for Tandembox (found in the routs folder).
  • DA: Assemble and attach all hardware including the tip on hardware.

Door application.

Tip on for doors:
Tip on for doors-use longer piston (38 mm extension).
The piston does not need a magnet attached to back of door.
2 hinges- The Doors can be hinged with std. blumotion hinges setup.
3 or more hinges- The Doors need the std. amount of blumotion hinges but all non-blumotion hinges need to be replaced with unsprung hinges.
The door will pop open enough to get your fingers in and pull open.
