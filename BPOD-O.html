BPOD-O
  • Drilling is only required on one gable.
  • System holes will be drilled to attach hardware.
  • The door must get a 5/8” thick hardware panel attached to the back of the door.
  • The hardware panel is predrilled for the DFS and for the BPOD.
Installation steps:
  • Attach DFS to hardware panel.
  • Attach door to hardware panel.
  • Attach brackets to hardware panel.
  • Attach brackets/hardware panel/door to the sliding bracket.
  • Adjust for level.
  • Check that the baskets fit into the cabinet while on the sliding hardware and that it all works smoothly. Shrink wrap them together. Label the baskets with the job# and cab, # and give them to shipping.
H = the full height of the sliding rack.

***Note that the minimum opening height numbers are plus 32mm (plus 16 at the top and the bottom) because our hardware panel requires us to move the hardware up and back 16mm.
