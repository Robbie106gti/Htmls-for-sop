Custom Stain order process for the Finishing Team

Custom stain order process when we “match to an item or sample” chunk of something.

The Finishing team will receive a custom stain “match to an item or sample chunk of something” request sheet from the order desk.

  1. On a new PO, note the:
    1. Current date.
    2. Required date (typically 3 working days).
    3. We will P/U (pick up).
    4. Our custom stain number.
    5. Order 1 liter of toner
    6. Order 1 liter of stain.
  2. Give the “item or sample chunk of something” to our purchasing team along with the yellow copy of the PO and three 6” x 10” blocks of the material we want stained, to be dropped off at Richelieu.
  3. Put the white copy of the PO into the purchasing office rack.
  4. Put the request sheet in the custom color request rack in the Finishing office.
  5. Pick up the custom stain when ready.
  6. When we receive the custom stain make 4 sample blocks to confirm the match.
    1. Throw the request sheet away.
    2. In the custom color program, note the Toner code, Stain code and any instructions.
    3. Get 3 custom color labels from Megan for the Dealers sample blocks.
    4. Give 3 sample blocks for the dealer and, along with the original “match to item”, to shipping.
    5. Make 1 sample block for Nickels and put it into the rack in the Finishing office with codes and instructions written on the back.

When ordering stain for a job:

15 parts = 2 gallons stain and 2 gallons toner

70 parts = 3 gallons stain and 3 gallons toner
