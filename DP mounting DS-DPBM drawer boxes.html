Steps:

  • Retrieve drawer box with matching job and cabinet number.

  • Attach left and right locking devices.

    • Butt against the side.

    • Set 1mm back from front.

    • Use two ¾” #6 screws.

  • Attach the DFS disc.

    • Use 16mm system screw.

    • Face the convex side against the drawer box.

    • Overhang the DFS disc on the drawer box, keeping the holes horizontal.

    • For DS-DPBM and DS-DMBM, leave a 1 to 2mm gap between the screw and the edge of the hole.

    • For DS-PDJBM, leave no gap between the screw and the edge of the hole.
