Multimedia shelf pullout thing ( rivaling the AV shelf )
  • This is a nice option to our AV shelf because it works on our standard tandem slide which means it’s soft close and hidden.
  • Scott’s find.
  • Note that the back of the shelf must have the rear fixing bracket for the tandem slide to fit into, SH-FB (T51.7000) is required. 2 per hardware pair. We are using this because a hole in the actual shelf may cause issues with potentially breaking through.
  • The drilling on the cabinet gable is the same height as a ROS but set back 6mm because of the 22mm front.
  • Minimum inside opening cabinet depth = hardware +28mm.
Process:
E
  • Order SH-FB,
  • Add to PA list
  • Enter parts into QuickCAM.
  • Add machining to the appropriate cabinet.
B
  • cut optimized parts.
PA
  • assemble.
F
  • finish.
FA
  • Attach SH-FB to shelf.
  • Attach slides to predrilled holes.
  • Install shelf.
