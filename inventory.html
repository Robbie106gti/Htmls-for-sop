Inventory
Inventoried items that are not ordered by the shop.

There are items that are on our inventory list but are not ordered by the shop. They are ordered by engineering on a per order basis.

They are on our inventory list because:

  1. Some of these items come in larger quantities than required (e.g. a roll of edgetape) so that there is leftover for future use.
  2. Some of these used to be ordered by the shop and we still have inventory remaining.

When the items that fall into the b) category have been used up, Jason will remove them from the inventory list.
