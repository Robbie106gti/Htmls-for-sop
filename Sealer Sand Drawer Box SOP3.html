Sealer Sand Drawer Box Process

Employees:

2 or 4

Required Time:

(Amount of drawer boxes) x 1.5 minutes + 1.5 minutes.

Position 1
Tools:
  • Palm sander with 280 grit sandpaper.
Goals:
  • Sand all necessary flat faces.
Steps:

  1. Sand all top edges of the drawer box.

  2. Sand the inside bottom of the drawer box.

  3. Sand the inside sides and ends of the drawer box.

  4. Sand the outside sides of the drawer box.

Position 2
Tools:
  • Foam sanding block wrapped with 320 grit foam sandpaper.
Goals:
  • Sand all inside corners and break the top edges.
  • Off load and on load the parts onto a rack.
Steps:

  1. Load position 1 with a drawer box (starting at the top of the rack).

  2. Sand all inside edges of the drawer box.

  3. Break the top edges of the drawer box.

  4. Rack the sanded drawer box and give position 1 the next drawer box.

  5. Repeat steps 1 to 4 until done.
