cookies
  • Bake cookies in toaster oven by reception desk.
  • Baking instructions:

    Preheat oven to 300 0F.
    Place frozen dough on to an ungreased baking sheet, allowing room for cookies to spread.
    Bake approximately 16 -17 minutes.
    Let cookies cool 15 minutes before removing from baking sheet.
    For Shortbread, preheat oven to 350F and bake approximately 15 minutes.

  • Storage instructions:

    Cookies are best kept in the freezer (up to 6 months) or in the refrigerator (up to 3 weeks). If we receive a big order of cookies, repackage the cookies into smaller Tupperware containers.

  • Time them for 15 minutes before clients arrive.
