Green sheets

  • These items are in house fix ups. They are not in Webquoin.

  • Do these items ASAP for the Date and Time required.

  • The item should be treated with care and protected as much as possible.

  • Whoever has to handle an item that has to be repaired/fixed, is responsible for applying and reapplying the appropriate protection to the item.

  • The Team leader of the team or a designated person within the team that notices the nonconformance is responsible for filling out Green sheets and making sure they continue to the next process via the next Team leader or Lead Hand.

  • The Team Leaders/Lead Hands are responsible for getting the item(s) done ASAP according to the date and time required and are responsible for making sure that the item(s) are given to the next department or process involved. If there is a Green Sheet item(s) that is taking longer then it should, let the appropriate people know what the problem is.

  • Always enter a date, type of issue and the team where you believe the issue started.

  • Either attach the item label to the sheet or fill in all the item information.

  • Keep the green sheet with the item until it has reached its final destination in our shop.

  • After completion, place the green sheets in the appropriate area for collection.

  • A designated QC person will review/troubleshoot.

Each green sheet will be valued at a $150 loss to Nickels.

  • The Team Leaders/Lead Hands are responsible for getting the item(s) done ASAP according to the date and time required and are responsible for making sure that the item(s) are given to the next department or process involved. If there is a Pink Sheet item(s) that is taking longer then it should, let the appropriate people know what the problem is.
