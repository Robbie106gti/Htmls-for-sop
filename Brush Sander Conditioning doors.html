F Brush Sander – Conditioning Doors SOP
Purpose:

To break all sharp edges on a door before painting. All orders must be pre-assessed to see if they need this process. If they have no sharp edges, then this step can be skipped.

Back:

Total Workers required = 2

1 = Operator
2 = Tailer

Job Descriptions:

Operator

  • Feed pieces constantly leaving a 6” to 7” space between the doors on all sides.

Tailer

  • Put on conveyor.

Front: Total Workers required = 2

1 = Operator
2 = Tailer

Job Descriptions:

Operator

  • Feed pieces constantly leaving a 6” to 7” space between the doors on all sides. Rack completed doors.

Tailer

  • Put on conveyor.
