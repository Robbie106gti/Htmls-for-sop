EP-D (End Panel with a door incorporated):

An FP gets a like sized door attached to it and all edges are veneered to make it look like one piece.

Standards:

  • Finished on all faces and edges.

  • The door must have a square edge detail.

  • Note that the thickness varies depending on the door thickness. The total thickness = a total of the FP (3/4” thick) and the door thickness. Typically, it is a total thickness between 1 ½” to 1 9/16”.

  • Labor for assembly in PA = 2 1/2 hours.

EP-DT (End Panel with a door incorporated and a mock toe kick attached at the bottom):

An FP gets a like sized door attached to it and all edges are veneered to make it look like one piece. An EP the size of the requested mock toekick is then biscuited onto the bottom with broken edges on the joint between the mock toekick and the EP-D.

  • Finished on all faces and edges.

  • The door must have a square edge detail.

  • The joint between the EP-D and the mock toe kick isn’t dowelled, it’s biscuited. There is no gap between the pieces but they have broken edges on the joint so the seam is visible.

  • Note that the thickness varies depending on the door thickness. The total thickness = a total of the FP (3/4” thick) and the door thickness. Typically, it is a total thickness between 1 ½” to 1 9/16”.

  • Labor for assembly in PA = 3 hours.
