Funkiness:

  1. We will only offer the grey option in webquoin since it is available in all sizes.
  2. Note that in the smallest size, the 42” and 43” cabinet, the front 2 baskets are just chrome with no grey shelf whereas in the other sizes, the front basket has a grey shelf.

Standards:

  • Available in all BB and BBFD cabinets.
  • Cabinet widths including and between 42” and 47”. Cabinets wider than 47” require a MC gable.
  • Minimum cabinet depth = 559mm (23”).
  • Minimum door width = 454mm (17 7/8”).
  • Minimum door height = 556mm ( 22 7/8”).
  • Minimum opening width = 457mm (18”).
  • Minimum opening height = 545mm (21 7/16”).
  • Drill the MC gable for the MC hardware. 152mm is the width for a 24” deep cabinet.
