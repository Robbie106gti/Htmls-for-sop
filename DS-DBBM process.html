Results after first order:

It is an awesome product compared to the DS-DMBM.
Only our standard trimming is required (14mm) on the panel saw as opposed to 90mm off the ends for the DS-DMBM because the supplied sheets are in such good condition.
No knots, tear out or bad material to cut around.
It is finished better than the DS-DMBM.
It is a little softer and has a better finish when it comes in so very minimal sanding is required on the ends and sides.
Dennis says it is beautiful to work with and the final product looks better.
The plywood birch bottoms match perfectly to the ends and sides whereas with the DS-DMBM it was always a little off.
