F Brush Sander –Painted MDF doors (e.g. Softline door) SOP

Back: Total Workers required = 4

1 = Operator
2 = QC
3,4 = Tailers

Job Descriptions:
Operator

  • eed 2 pieces every 20 seconds, adjusting this pace as per QC’s instruction.

Tailers

  1. Keep pieces flat. QC back face, only sanding out any bumps. Use “super fine” sanding block.

  2. Any defects (something that can’t be repaired in 20 seconds), put aside and fix after all parts are completed.

  3. Give to QC

QC

  • Final check and put on conveyor.

After completion, the entire crew (at the discretion of QC) works on fixing all the parts with defects.

Front: Total Workers required = 6

1 = Operator
2 = QC
3,4,5,6 = Tailers

Job Descriptions:
Operator

  • Feed 2 pieces every 30 seconds, adjusting this pace as per QC’s instruction.

Tailers 3 & 4

  1. Sand outside edges and QC front face, only sanding out any bumps. Use “super fine” sanding block.

  2. Any defects (something that can’t be repaired in 20 seconds), put aside and fix after all parts are completed.

Tailers 5 & 6

  1. Sand inside profile. Use “super fine” sanding block.

  2. Give to QC

QC

  • Final check and put on conveyor.

After completion, the entire crew (at the discretion of QC) works on fixing all the parts with defects.
