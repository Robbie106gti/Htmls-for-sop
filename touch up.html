Re-order paint, when we have < ¼ of a gallon remaining.

The process is:

We supply a touch-up bottle (60ml bottle with brush in the cap) filled with the pre-catalyzed paint.There is no need to provide catalyst or thinner to the order.

For all the other colors, custom or standard, the process is:
  1. Paint
    Process (Presidium - 60ml bottles w/ brush in the cap):
    • Fill up with the required finish.
    • Supply 50ml bottle with catalyst (399-5005).
    • Supply mixing instructions for Presidium finishes (Touch Up Bottles).
    Process (Titan – 60ml bottles w/ brush in the cap):
    • Fill up with the required finish.
    • Supply 50ml bottle with catalyst (399-5020-00)
    • Supply mixing instructions for Titan finishes (Touch Up Bottles).
    Process (Presidium – 1 Litre / 1 Quart):
    • Fill a Litre / quart with the required finish
    • Supply both catalyst (399-5005) and the EZ-Thinner in 250ml bottles
    • Supply mixing instructions for Presidium finishes (1 Litre / Quart).
    Process (Titan – 1 Litre / 1 Quart):
    • Fill a Litre / quart with the required finish
    • Supply both catalyst (399-5020-00) and the Ethanol in 250ml bottles
    • Supply mixing instructions for Titan finishes (1 Litre / Quart).
  2. Stain
    Process (Toner and Stain – Touch up bottles and Litre / Quart)
    • Fill one container with 60% of the requested volume of toner.
    • Fill another container with 40% of the requested volume of stain.
    Process (Stain only – Touch up bottles and Litre / Quart)
    • Fill the container of the requested volume with stain.
  3. Clear Coat / Clear Lacquer (Ultraguard)

    Process

    • Fill the container for the requested volume with the Lacquer;
    • Supply the same sized container with a small amount of catalyst (399-5005). Only 10% is necessary.
    • Supply mixing instructions for clear coats (Ultraguard).

Mixing Instructions – Touch Up Bottle
60 ml (Presidium)
  1. Before adding Catalyst, shake the finish well (paint), vigorously for 3 minutes.
  2. To the original volume of finish (paint), add:
    • 20% of Catalyst
  3. Mix well for one minute

e.g. For 60 ml. of paint, add 12 ml. of catalyst.

Mixing Instructions – Touch Up Bottle >50 ml (Titan)
  1. Before adding Catalyst, shake the finish well (paint), vigorously for 3 minutes.
  2. To the original volume of finish (paint), add:
    • 10% of Catalyst
  3. Mix well for one minute

e.g. For 50 ml. of paint, add 5 ml. of catalyst.

Mixing Instructions – Litre / Quart Paint (Presidium)
  1. Before adding Catalyst, shake the finish well (paint), vigorously for 3 minutes.
  2. To the original volume of finish (conversion varnish / lacquer), add:
    1. 17% of Thinner
    2. 18% of Catalyst
  3. Mix well for one minute

e.g. For 1000 ml. (1 Litre) of paint, add 170 ml. of thinner and 180 ml. of catalyst.

Mixing Instructions – Litre / Quart Paint (Titan)
  1. Before adding Catalyst, shake the finish well (paint), vigorously for 3 minutes.
  2. To the original volume of finish (conversion varnish / lacquer), add:
    1. 5% of Ethanol
    2. 12% of Catalyst
  3. Mix well for one minute.

e.g. For 1000 ml. (1 Litre) of paint, add 50 ml. of thinner and 120 ml. of catalyst.

Mixing Instructions – Touch Up Bottle / Litre / Quart Clear Coat – Ultraguard)
  1. Before adding Catalyst, shake the finish well (lacquer), vigorously for 3 minutes.
  2. To the original volume of finish (conversion varnish / lacquer), add 10% of Catalyst.
  3. Mix well for one minute.

e.g. For 1000 ml. (1 Litre) of Clear Coat, add 100 ml. of catalyst.
