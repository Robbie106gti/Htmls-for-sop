Purchasing:

  1. ASK FOR SALES DESK.
  2. TELL THEM IT IS NICKELS CUSTOM CABINETS CALLING.
  3. GIVE PART NUMBER AND QUANTITY.
  4. ASK FOR A PRICE ON EACH ITEM. COMPARE PRICE TO QUOTES FOR ACCURACY. BE AWARE OF DIFFERENCES BETWEEN MEASURING SCALES.
  5. TELL THEM IF IT IS FOR PICK UP OR DELIVERY.
  6. IF IT IS FOR PICK UP, ASK THEM IF EVERYTHING IS IN STOCK.
  7. GIVE THEM A PURCHASE ORDER NUMBER.
  8. TELL THEM THANKS, AND HAVE A NICE DAY.
  9. IF YOU FAX AN ORDER, MAKE SURE YOU ASK THEM TO FAX BACK A CONFIRMED PICK UP OR DELIVERY DATE AND A PRICE FOR EACH ITEM ORDERED. COMPARE PRICE TO QUOTES FOR ACCURACY. BE AWARE OF DIFFERENCES BETWEEN MEASURING SCALES.

Deliverer process:

Honk the forklift horn twice.

Receiving:

  • The first team head that sees a deliverer waiting, should:
  • Approach him with a smile and ask him if he has been helped.
  • Check that the correct amount of packages have arrived according to his paperwork.
  • Ensure that the package isn’t damaged. If it is, you need to note that on the receiving work and you and the deliverer need to sign it.
  • If the Receiver is unavailable but is IN (See the Receiver sign):
    • Leave the item on the Receivers table along with the paperwork.
    • Notify the appropriate team head if it is material that needs to be unloaded with a forklift.
  • If the Receiver is OUT (See the Receiver sign):
    • Deliver the item to the appropriate team head and leave the paperwork on the Receivers desk. The Team head should confirm, after opening the package, that type, quantity etc. are correct. DWD moulding and large Blum hardware orders are exceptions and must be left for the receiver to check off. If any items are taken from 1 of these exceptions before Receiving has checked them off, leave a note stating this.
    • Notify the appropriate team head if it is material that needs to be unloaded with a forklift.

Notes:

  • Don't put "one of" items with a receipt into PP.
  • Request items to be received on date requested by E, except Blum and Upper Canada.
  • Inventory items requested by the shop can typically arrive the next day except Blum.
  • Check received items within 24 hours of receiving.
  • Close received items within 24 hours of receiving.
  • Check for double freight charges on the same truck (Upper Canada).
  • Keep involved teams aware of what's going on.
  • When possible, keep inventory at < a months supply.
