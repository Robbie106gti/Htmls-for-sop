The code in Quoin is DM-POCKET.

Pocket door hardware <48” high -1321 Pro Pocket
Standards:
  • The hardware package includes all hardware (hinges and mounting plates, etc.)
  • The pocket door frame may be required above or below the pocket doors in certain scenarios.
  • Doors overlap the side gables our standard 14.5mm.
  • The hardware attaches onto the top and bottom shelf.
  • This hardware has an Inner gable.
Limits:
  • The cabinet opening depth is greater than 16”.
  • The hardware length options are: 16” to 28” on 2” increments.
  • The door height is > 17 1/8” and < 48”.
  • The hardware requires 3 ¼” from the outside of the cabinet to work.
  • Minimum door style width of 2 1/4”. Glass doors need a minimum style width of 1 ¾”.
  • Not available with the Verona, Venice, Milano, Naples and Seville doors.
The door size is:

width = standard as doors on a std. Cabinet. Height = opening – 8mm. When in the pocket position, the door is 4 ½” short of the back of the hardware.

Door drilling . The tab

(distance from door edge to hinge hole) = 12.5mm. A 40mm hinge hole is drilled at a hole center of 32.5mm from the door side edge and 98mm from the door end.

Inner gable is the same color as the cabinet interior.

Amount = 1 per door. Height = opening height minus 6mm. Depth = opening depth – 3”.

The follower (hinge joining piece).

It is black melamine. The size of the follower is 16mm th. x 102mm W x (opening height minus 279mm) H.

The door stopper :

It’s the same color as the cabinet interior. The size is 16mm th. x 38mm L. x 16mm H. It is finished on 5 sides.

Plastic line up guide for our standard 7/8” thick door = the 7/8” line.

Pocket door hardware >48” high –1432 hardware
Standards:
  • This is a stronger setup for larger doors.
  • These doors don’t overlap the side gables.
  • The hardware attaches onto the side gables.
  • The pocket door frame may be required above, on the sides or below the pocket doors in certain scenarios.
Limits:
  • The cabinet opening depth is greater then 16”.
  • The door height is > 48”. The hardware requires 3” from the inside of the cabinet gable to work.
  • Minimum door style width of 2 1/4”.
  • The hardware is available from 16” to 28” long in 2” increments.
  • Not available with the Verona, Venice, Milano, Naples and Seville doors.
The door size is:

Width and height = opening – 9mm.

Door drilling .

A 35mm diameter hinge hole is drilled at a hole center of 25.5mm from the door side edge and 47mm from the door end. A hinge hole must be added at the center of the door.

Guide block

must be installed flush with the front of the track.
