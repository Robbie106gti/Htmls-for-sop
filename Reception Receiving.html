Goals:

  • No inventory or repair items piling up in the showroom.

Whenever possible, reception should send all delivery people to our receiving team. Note that there is yellow sign by the receiving door.

These are the steps we will follow when items are received by our receptionist.

Items for repair:

  • Reception will take appropriate information: Dealer name, customer name, order #, required repair.
  • Put the item(s) in the pre-sprinkler room.
  • Email the information to the Order desk ( orderdesk@nickelscabinets.com ) and the Production Manager.
  • Order desk should create an order for the repair and notify Purchasing/Receiving if the item needs to be moved to the tunnel.

Inventory Items:

  • Reception will take appropriate information: Ensure that the packing slip matches the package and note whether the package is damaged or not with the delivery company.
  • Put the item(s) in the pre-sprinkler room.
  • Notify Purchasing/Receiving that the item is there.
  • None of these items should remain in the sprinkler room for more than 24 hours.

Color samples:

  • Reception will take appropriate information: Dealer name, customer name and order #.
  • Email the information to the Order desk ( orderdesk@nickelscabinets.com ) about the item.
  • Order desk will immediately pick up the sample from reception and process an order.
  • Order desk will put the sample in the pre-sprinkler room.
  • The sample remains in the sprinkler room until needed by the shop.
  • When the order is complete, the sample is either discarded or returned to the dealer depending on what the order specifies.
