How to change the blade.

  • Turn off the blades and wait for the blades to stop moving.
  • Loosen the blade coupling with the allen key.
  • Remove the coupling.
  • Remove the blade washer.
  • Remove the blade.
  • Insert the new blade, blade washer and coupling.
  • Tighten the coupling.

Note that it is the same procedure for both blades.

How to adjust the blade positoning.

  • Place a piece of scrap material on the blade path.
  • Turn both blades on.
  • Lift the foot bar.
  • Inspect the sample cut and move the blade position accordingly using the allen key.
  • Inspect the sample cut and raise or lower the scoring blade position accordingly using the allen key.
  • Measure the blade width.
  • Email engineering the blade width.
  • Good to go.
