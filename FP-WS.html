FP-WS1 (Wainscot 1)

  • If they request 1” of scribe, here is what we’ll do :
    • If a 36” high Wainscot 1 was requested, an extra 1” would be added onto the bottom for scribe. They will actually receive a 37” high panel and the bottom rail will be 6” high instead of 5” high.
    • If a 36” high Wainscot 1 with a mock kick was requested, an extra 1” would be added onto the bottom for scribe. They will actually receive a 37” high panel and the mock kick area would be 1” higher (5 ½”).

FP-WS2 (Wainscot 2)

  • If they request 1” of scribe, here is what we’ll do:
    • If a 36” high Wainscot 2 was requested, an extra 1” would be added onto the bottom for scribe. They will actually receive a 37” high panel and the bottom rail will be 5” high instead of 4” high.
    • If a 36” high Wainscot 2 with a mock kick was requested, an extra 1” would be added onto the bottom for scribe. They will actually receive a 37” high panel and the mock kick area would be 1” higher (5 ½”).
  • V joints. The joints have broken edges.
  • The back edges of the Rails must be broken on the inside edge of the panels.
  • We will now use the same material for WS2 as we do for the PROMO-PSB (sample blocks).
  • The WS2 width is 4" and the thickness is 5/16".
