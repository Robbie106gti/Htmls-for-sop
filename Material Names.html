Material names SOP (Name/code)

WM-16-CH
This means white melamine on 1 side, cherry veneer on the other side, 8' long and 5/8" thick. This material will get trimmed in the length.
WM-16L-CH
The L represents material that is 10' long and won't get trimmed in the length (so we can cut full length material).
WM-16T-CH
The T represents material that is 10' long. This material will get trimmed in the length.
WM-16U-CH
The U represents material that is 8' long and won't get trimmed in the length (so we can cut full length material).
WM-16E-CH
The E represents enviro core material. This material will get trimmed in the length.
WM-16M-CH
The M represents MDF core material (needed for Beaded items). This material will get trimmed in the length.
WM-16PL-CH
The PL represents plywood core material. This material will get trimmed in the length.
