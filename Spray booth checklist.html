Startup:

  • QC and assemble guns.
  • If gun “O” ring is damaged, replace.
  • Clogs must be cleaned with the Mohawk tool.
  • Clean floor and filters.

Spraying procedure checks

  • Tip size.

  • Air pressure.

  • Fluid pressure.

  • Clean parts.

  • Spray start + stop.

  • Triggering.

  • Gun distance.

  • Gun angle.

  • Spray overlap.

  • Spray pass amounts.

  • Agitator in use.

  • Sample being used.

  • Next rack ready as per schedule.

If free time occurs:

Fill up touch up bottles. If you haven't had time to do this (2 days), let the Spray Booth coordinator know.

Wednesday morning before 7:30 AM.

  • Fill the pump flushing thinner container, to 3/4 full.

Shutdown:

  • Flush pump.
  • Clean all pressure pots and lines.
  • QC and disassemble guns.
  • Tips into thinner.
  • Make air cap clean.
  • Hang the gun upright.
  • Dispose of all catalized material.
  • Left over lacquer for drawer boxes.
