Standards
  • Material options = Baltic Birch plywood (ROS-DP-TD) & Solid Birch (ROS-DB-TD).
  • Space = see chart.
  • Maximum depth = 756mm.
  • Minimum depth = 456mm.
  • When a gable isn’t hinged, a spacer will not be used for the ROS Tray divider.
  • When a cabinet depth is increased beyond the depth of 24”, the depth of the ROS Tray divider does not increase unless charged for and noted.
ROS Spacer
  • We’ll use 2 spacers per slide.
Standard location:
  • If 1 ROS Tray divider is added in any cabinet, it is placed at the bottom of the cabinet.
  • If more than 1 is added to a cabinet, one is at the bottom and the rest are evenly spaced in the cabinet. Any adjustable shelves are evenly spaced above the ROS Tray dividers.
Process:
  • E
    • Custom – Highlight for optimizing.
    • Add a 12” High ROS in QuickCAM.
    • Add appropriate amount of dividers.
    • Add parts for nesting.
    • Make program for machining. Including curves and dados.
  • B
    • Nest.
  • DA
    • Assemble. Pin from back and bottom only.
  • F
    • Finish
  • FA
    • Install
Price:

Price of 1 ROS tray divider = (1 x cost of a 12” H. ROS) + (4 x (amount of dividers x (cost of 1 divider)))
