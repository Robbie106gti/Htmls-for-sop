Repairs/Fixes/Fixups/Problems/Remakes/Unusual/Bizarre/weird/unique
Standards:

  • The situation must be clearly stated in Quoin or in the tracking status notes.

  • The situation must be clearly stated on an orange cover sheet that goes with the order through the shop.

  • The item should be treated with care and protected as much as possible. Protect the item with whatever is appropriate (cardboard or green tape) so that more damage isn’t caused.

  • Whoever has to handle an item that has to be repaired/fixed, is responsible for applying and reapplying the appropriate protection to the item.

O

  • Ensure that the entire problem is pre-investigated and clearly stated in Quoin.

  • Give the item a price in Quoin.

E

  • Ensure that the entire problem is clearly stated on all paperwork and that the tracking status is marked orange for all required teams.

  • Fill in the tracking status, price and amount.

Team heads:

  • Put an orange cover sheet for all effected parts so that cover sheet follows the parts through the shop.

  • Make sure these orders are personally monitored from beginning to end, engaging all that is required to get it done right.

  • You aren’t allowed to change the tracking status from orange to green until you have personally checked the item(s) for quality, ensured that the situation is correct and personally passed the item(s) off to the next team head.

  • Put your Initials and date in the tracking status, signifying that you have completed the item according to Nickels standards.

E.g. Parts for Finishing, parts for Doors, parts for PA, parts for DA, parts for Millwork.
