  • We order 5/8”, ¾”and 3/16” sheet goods from Hardwoods or it will be laid up in our shop.

  • Veneer and Edge tape will be ordered from Hardwoods.

  • Veneer will be the Echo Wood brand.

  • Free standing gables will be exotic on both faces when selected (eg GBLHG-FSG-2XF-SQ).

  • Available in a Natural finish or with a custom stain with a Satin or Matte sheen.

  • A washcoat is too be used to seal the open grain whenever we are finishing these exotic veneers with a Natural finish: EW RC White Oak, EW QC Walnut, EW QC Macassar Ebony, EW QC Zebrawood, EW QC Teak, EW QC Mahogany, EW QC Wenge, EW Gun Metal Ebony.

  • DE edge detail is NA.

  • Solid material is NA.

  • Some parts (eg filler) may be either ¾” or 5/8” thick depending on availability.

  • The material will be named on the shop material copies like this:

  • EX-16-WM -This means exotic grain is vertical on the material.

  • EX H-16-WM -The H means the exotic grain is horizontal (grain runs in the direction of the width) on the material.

Doors

  • Available doors are:Alpha VGM, Alpha HG, Urban VGM, Urban HG, Sierra VGM and Sierra HG and they have a matching back.

  • The doors will be ¾” material.

  • The edges will be veneered with a matching veneer edge tape.

  • Nickels will make the doors. The grain will run in the same direction on the back as the front of the piece.
