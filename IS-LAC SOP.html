IS-LAC coverage, contents and application
Coverage:

Sealer = 24 sq. ft. per litre

Topcoat = 18 sq. ft. per litre

Paint Application

Lacquer (IS-LAC) is only needed if glaze is involved.

Mixing instructions: Lacquer

Add 3.5% catalyst to Lacquer:

eg. With 1000 ml (1litre) of lacquer add 35 ml of catalyst.

Matte contents:

We supply Lacquer and 3.5% catalyst.

e.g. 1 Litre of IS-LAC = 1000 ml (1litre) of lacquer, and 35 ml of catalyst.

Satin contents:

We supply Lacquer and 3.5% catalyst.

e.g. 1 Litre of IS-LAC = 1000 ml (1litre) of lacquer, and 35 ml of catalyst.

Old Stain application.

IS-LAC (Lacquer)
The lacquer comes with catalyst/thinner. The catalyst/thinner that is supplied is premeasured according to the amount of lacquer that is requested. An equal proportion of both should be used. eg. If you decide to only use half of the lacquer, you only need half of the catalyst/thinner.
Once the catalyst/thinner is added to the lacquer, you have 72 hours to use it.
Catalyst & Thinner Not Needed When Ordering Lacquer In The "VIRIDIAN LINE"

We need to know if they want the Matte or Satin finish.
We send the same amount of lacquer as we do paint or stain.
