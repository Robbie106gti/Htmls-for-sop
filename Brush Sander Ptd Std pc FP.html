F Brush Sander –Painted/Stained, 5 Piece, Flat Panel, SQ profile (e.g. shaker door) SOP

Pre-assess wood doors to ensure this process is valid. If they are to rough, this step is unnecessary and they must all be done manually.

Back: Total Workers required = 4

1 = Operator
2 = QC
3,4 = Tailers

Job Descriptions:

Operator

  • Feed 2 pieces every 20 seconds, adjusting this pace as per QC’s instruction.

Tailers

  1. Keep pieces flat and feel for bumps on the back frame with 1 hand.

  2. Sand the entire center panel and inside edge, but only sand out any bumps on the frame. Use “super fine” sanding block.

  3. Any defects (something that can’t be repaired in 20 seconds), put aside and fix after all parts are completed.

  4. Give to QC

QC

  • Final check and put on conveyor.

After completion, the entire crew (at the discretion of QC) works on fixing all the parts with defects.

Front: Total Workers required = 8

1 = Operator
2 = QC
3,4,5,6,7,8 = Tailers

Job Descriptions:

Operator

  • Feed 2 pieces every 20 seconds, adjusting this pace as per QC’s instruction.

Tailers 3 and 4

  1. Keep pieces flat and sand outside edges. Use “super fine” sanding block.

  2. QC front frame, only sanding out any bumps.

  3. Any defects (something that can’t be repaired in 20 seconds), put aside and fix after all parts are completed.

  4. Give to next station.

Tailers 5 and 6

  1. Keep pieces flat, sand inside edge. Use “super fine” sanding block.

  2. Any defects (something that can’t be repaired in 20 seconds), put aside and fix after all parts are completed.

  3. Give to next station.

Tailers 7 and 8

  1. Keep pieces flat and sand entire center panel, using extra care around the edges because of scuffing caused by sanding the inside profile. Use “super fine” sanding block.

  2. Any defects (something that can’t be repaired in 20 seconds), put aside and fix after all parts are completed.

  3. Give to QC

QC
  • Final check and put on conveyor.

After completion, the entire crew (at the discretion of QC) works on fixing all the parts with defects.
