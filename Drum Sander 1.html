To turn on:
  • Press Main Power button.
  • Turn on Sanding Belt #1
  • Wait until a sharp bump displays on the gauge above the button
  • Turn on Sanding Belt #2
  • Turn on Table Feed
  • Control Table Feed speed with the Table feed Speed Dial accordingly
  • Open Vacuum
To turn off:
  • Press Main Power button

    • OR

  • Press Emergency Stop button
  • Close Vacuum
