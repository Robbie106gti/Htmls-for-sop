Moisture Content SOP

Goal:

  • To send out doors that are what was wanted and will not fail.

Rules:

  • Check moisture content of doors when they arrive to our Door Team.

  • Take 5 readings and use the average.

  • 10 is optimal, 8 - 11 is acceptable.

  • Mark Job# on list if acceptable.

  • If the doors aren't acceptable, keep them in the Door Team area for 3 days to acclimatize.

  • Chek moisture content of doors to confirm they have corrected when they leave our Door Team.
