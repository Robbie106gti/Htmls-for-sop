Grinder SOP

a.k.a. chipper

To Turn On :

  1. Turn master switch on (right to 12 o’clock).
  2. Press Green button.
  3. Press white button 3 (right of the green button) so light is on.

To Turn Off :

  1. If grinder is grinding, push button 3 so light is off.
  2. Turn master switch off (left to 9 o’clock).

If the emergency button has been pushed :

Before doing anything else, you will have to pull the emergency button out.

Grinder Standards :

  • Maximum width of 450mm (17 5/8”) x 1829mm (6’)
  • Cut 9mm plywood into < 6” strips for the grinder.
  • Keep a record of the teeth changing/rotating dates so that we know what we’ve been doing and can determine what is a good change time frame.
  • If the machine gets jammed or stops chipping (can’t hear chips going up the pipe), the first potential solution is to wedge a piece between the pusher and the material (dunnage works great) and let the machine do the work.
