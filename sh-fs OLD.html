Installation:

The side marked as the top must be facing up.

Install the rail to the wall using the supplied screws and attaching the rail to studs or to the supplied drywall anchors.

Slide the shelf into position. It is recommended that you add a bead of silicone onto the supports to hold the shelf into position.
