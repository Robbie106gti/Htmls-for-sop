Standards

Plywood drawer/rollout shelf organizer.

Made by DA.

3/8” thick plywood.

MSC-DOP – Plywood with a clear lacquer finish.

MSC-DOS – Plywood with a silver paint finish.

The length and width of the squares are all equal and as close to 100mm without going under.

The spaces on the sides are equal and as close to 100mm without going under.

How to determine the size of the square:

  1. Determine how many squares will fit into the depth of the box keeping the square size as close to 100mm without going under.
  2. Determine how many squares will fit into the width keeping the side spaces equal and as close to 100mm without going under.
