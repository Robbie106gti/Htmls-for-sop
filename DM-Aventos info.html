DM-Aventos installing info:

Note that these adjustments work for all Aventos styles.

  • When attaching the hardware to the gables.

    • Remove transportation clips and discard.

    • Adjust the tension immediately because it’s easier to adjust while it’s in this position.

      Hardware

      Amount to adjust tension

      HF

      ¾”

      HK 2300

      3/8”

      HK 2500

      ¾”

      HL

      ¾”

      HL 2500

      ½”

      HS

      ¾”

    • Adjust the tension using a drill with a PZ2 bit. Note that this is a gear setup and not a drill\screw setup. The angle of the drill bit is the key. By watching the adjustment guide, it will be obvious when you have it in the correct position. See photo.

  • Leave instructions inside the cabinet for the site installer.

  • Adjust the hardware after the doors are installed.

  • Attach the cap plastic cover cap in house after the final adjustments.
