A European style of cabinet with fingerpull or without pulls/handles.

Standards.
  • See wide cabinet and Modal wide cabinet block SOP
  • No FE’s
  • Always proud gables and fillers.
  • Filler Overlays (FO), End Panels (EP) and Applied Gables (AG) need to hang below the cabinet by ½” when they are beside a Wall cabinet, to match the doors.
  • Blind cab’s get the return notched for the channel to match the width of the door.
  • The leg leveler is LL-140 from Richelieu. We stock these.
  • Install the L and C channels on site. Supply requested length + 6”.
  • Always send installation instructions with every order.
  • If cutting the channels in shop, use the big miter saw in PA using the special blade. Add wax to the blade.
  • An Allen Key will not be supplied by us.
  • Supply an end cap for every L and C channel end.
  • Fillers don’t have channels.
  • If a cabinet is at the end of a peninsula/run with the L channel running along the side also, attach a ½” thick spacer (3/4” back from the front and back of the cabinet). at the top of the gable making it obvious for the installer. Make a note on the spacer “ Do not to remove”.
  • No inside corners except BC’s.
  • NA: BAC, WAC, BD3D, BCRD, B angled front, BAF2D.
  • Modal support Bracket:
    • 1 bracket is required for all BSFD and VSFD with double doors, and any base cabinet that is wider than 35”.
    • 1 bracket needs 1) 5/8” screws, 1) ¼” nut, 1) ½” long x ¼”, Large flathead bolt (ground down).
    • Bob makes the L bracket and supplies all nuts, bolts and screws.
    • The bracket will be attached to the stretcher in shop by FA with the bolts attached.
Modifications:
  • To modify doors to extend them above a cabinet (used in a stacking scenario):
    1. Use the CC-B7 pricing
    2. Move drilling on the cabinet to match the door which will have drilling at the standard distance from the end.
  • To modify cabinet heights:

    Use CC-D6

  5. Ask Dealer re. end caps by stoves, DW’s etc. Do they want gables or end caps?
  6. Filler Overlays (FO), End Panels (EP) and Applied Gables (AG) need to hang below the cabinet by ½” when they are beside a Wall cabinet, to match the doors.
  7. Heavy duty Tandem slides need to be ordered per modal job.
  8. Stabilising hardware for tandem needs to be ordered per modal job.
  9. Heavy duty Tandembox and Tandembox needs to be ordered per job.
  10. Tandembox front stabiliser needs to be ordered per job.
  11. Stabilising hardware for tandembox should be ordered per job.
  12. On the ship list must be an installation instructions item.
  13. Supply a copy of the Installation instructions to shipping.
  14. Filler Overlays (FO), End Panels (EP) and Applied Gables (AG) need to hang below the cabinet by ½” when they are beside a Wall cabinet, to match the doors.
Installation:
  • Break the edge of the channels by running metal along the edge (eg. A drill bit).
  • The channels should either be cut with a triple chip blade or put wax on a regular blade.
  • Recommended that you use 2 sided thick tape to attach the Filler Overlay to the filler so it matches the depth of the door.
