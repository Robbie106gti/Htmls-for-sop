Painted Parts Finishing Processes
Goals:

  1. Achieve a finish that meets our requirements.

  2. Consistency.

  3. Make the Brush Sander do as much of the sanding as possible.

Key Points for the Sanders:
  • Light putty.
  • When sanding edges with an orbital, keep the door flat on the bench.
