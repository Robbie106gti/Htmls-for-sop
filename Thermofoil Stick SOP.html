We are going to eliminate FE’s from Lighthouse thermofoil jobs because:.

Thermofoil Lighthouse MTF FE’s

An FE application is when we take melamine and laminate a sheet of thermofoil onto it.

An FE takes about 30 minutes more labor to lay up the material then material for an FE in the custom line. This is because of the thin/fragile (marks/creases easily) material and the difficulty in handling it.

The inconsistent surface is what it is and doesn’t add much time. A minute is spent flattening out bad spots with a hammer/back side of a nail set.
