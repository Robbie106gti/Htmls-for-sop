Turning off:

Push the Off button.
Step on the brake (or else it will keep spinning for a long time).

Changing the blade:

Lock out the machine.
Lock the drive shaft.
Use the large wrenches to loosen the blade nuts.
Put in the new blade and retighten.
Put the drive shaft into neutral so the blade can spin freely and guesstimate with a sample where you want the height and depth adjusted to.
After the height and depth have been set, with the drive shaft still in neutral, ensure that the blade can still spin freely without hitting the fence etc.
Make sure the direction is correct.
Put drive shaft in gear, unlock switch, you are good to go.
