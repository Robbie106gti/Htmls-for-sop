Setup:

  • Tip = 09 114

  • Fluid Pressure = 30 PSI when the trigger is pulled.

  • Air Pressure = 15 PSI when the trigger is pulled.

  • Double check and make sure spray fan looks good. If it doesn’t, ask to see what is wrong.

Steps: NOTE that there are 3 applications of the topcoat.

  • Clean item(s) to be sprayed.

  • 1 pass on the edge(s) (if there is an edge(s) that requires finishing) and the face.

  • Spin rack.

  • 1 pass on the edge(s) (if there is an edge that requires finishing) and the face.

  • If you see any problems, stop and notify the team head.

  • Repeat these steps after drying for the second coat.

Guidelines:

  • Start and stop spraying off the piece.

  • Triggering (release trigger after every pass) is required.

  • Keep the gun tip 6” to 8” off the piece when spraying.

  • Gun spray should be held at a 30 degree angle when spraying edges.

  • Gun spray should be held at a 90 degree angle when spraying faces. NO ARCING.

  • Spray overlap should be 50%.
