Here is the method we will be using for Horizontal Grain application with ETM (European textured melamine), UPG (UV Painted Gloss) and UWG (UV Woodgrain Gloss) materials.

We will be using dominos.
  1. <30”, drill on the dowel inserter.
  2. >30”, use the Domino joiner.
    1. Check the joint and make sure it is acceptable.
    2. Glue the dominos into one of the pieces only.
    3. Join on site with glue and clamps.
