Guidelines For Healthy Computer Usage

The invasion of computer viruses, Trojans, worms, spam, pop-ups and other undesirables, necessitate to adhering to some user rules, so that our computers can function properly.

  • While browsing the Internet, only go to sights that are related too our business.
  • Never download from the internet any utilities, free programs, games or offers of cheap travel, accommodations or other products.
  • Never give out your email address to anyone except our customers or suppliers.

By following these guidelines, you will have your computer running smoothly and efficiently. Remember, the computer on your desk has not been placed there for your amusement. It is a tool, just as a saw or an edge bander, and it is your duty to keep it in good operating order.

Maintenance
  • Run and install all the updates when prompted. That Windows Updates, Norton Anti-irus and Ad-Aware.
  • Clean up all your cookies and temporary internet files. In internet explorer, tools, internet options, general tab, delete cookies, delete files.
  • Delete the correspondence that you no longer require and empty your recycle bin.
  • Once a month, check your hard drive: In windows explorer, click on “My Computer”, right click on the “C” drive, properties, tools, check now, select both options, select “YES” to being done on next start up.
