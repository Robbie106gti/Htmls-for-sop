DM-AVNT-HF (Aventos)
Nickels standards for this product :

  • Note that the METRO-ALBR and the METRO-ALMAT need custom machining on the sides. The door company we buy these from will do it for us as long as we tell them about it.

  • The gables are to be predrilled.

  • The adjustable shelf maximum depth should be cabinet depth from 22mm.

  • The holes drilled on the top door are standard drilling size but drilled on the top edge of the door and using hinges 70T5550.TL (120 degrees). The mounting plate holes on the bottom of this door will be drilled by the “D” team, using the supplied NCC template.

  • The holes drilled on the bottom door are standard drilling size but drilled on the top edge of the door and using hinges 78Z5500T. The hardware holes on the sides of this door will be drilled by the “D” team, using the supplied Blum template.

  • Engineering should use the blum Dynalog program to find what items are required for your situation.

  • For the HF, the hardware required will end up being a version of the following.

    1. Lift mechanism dependant on the power factor.

    2. Telescopic arm dependant on the cabinet height.

    3. 1 pair of hinges 70T5550.TL (120 degrees)

    4. 1 pair of hinges 78Z5500T.

  • Different material weights:

    1. MDF-1.3kg. per sq/ft.

    2. Alder-.85kg. per sq/ft.

    3. Maple-1.25kg. per sq/ft.

    4. Cherry-1.2kg. per sq/ft.

    5. K3 core-1.2kg. per sq/ft.
