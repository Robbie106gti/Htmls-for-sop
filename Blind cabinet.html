  • The Blind panel: the entire exterior face is finished to match the cabinet exterior.

  • The Blind panel is drilled for dowels (top and bottom) on the CNC router.

  • The back of The Blind panel is drilled for mounting plates and adj. Shelf clips on the CNC router and the material matches the interior of the cabinet.

  • The Blind Panel is attached with dowels and glue (It is clamped until the glue is dry). Staple through the gable into the blind panel to secure it from the side.

  • Blind panel = cabinet width – door width – 3mm per door.

  • The Blind Panel on a Wall cabinet needs the bottom edge taped also.

  • The P2P operator must check the plan for the size of the blind notch.

  • The front stretcher(s) and Top and Bottom shelf are drilled for dowels on the CNC router and notched to accept the return panel.

  • The adj. Shelf depth = cabinet depth – 35mm.

  • The doors hinges are:

    • For 16mm thick- HINGE-BLC (79T9980.37 Blum or 79T998180 Richelieu) (std. drilling).

    • For 16mm thick- Mounting plates = HINGE-MP45. Use the 175H916180 (6mm (9mm high)).

  • The 2nd front stretcher on a Base cabinet with a drawer box will match the top front stretcher but without the dowel holes.

  • If there is a cover shelf in the blind cabinet, it is treated as other cabinets with a cover shelf (Sent loose unless it is an open cabinet).
