Rover B / Klever Tasks

  • Green sheets take priority.

  • The team leader will tell you which machine is the priority for you.

  • Keep the “priority” machine moving.

  • Machine a minimum of 1 batch (1 rack of finished cabinet parts and 1 rack of custom melamine parts) a day.

  • Custom cabinets

    • Check that all parts are present and correct.

    • Dry fit when required.

    • Join up any finished cabinet parts off of the custom rack with the finished parts rack and supply all required paperwork.

Edgebander / Dowel inserter Tasks

  • Green sheets take priority.

  • Focus on taping and dowelling a minimum of a full cycle (production day) of parts. Doors, PA, Finished cabinet parts, Cabinet parts.

  • Cabinet part racks:

    • Make sure that all paperwork is present.

    • Make sure that all parts are present.

    • Match stretchers according to cabinet.

  • Finished cabinet part racks:

    • Make sure that all paperwork is present.

    • Make sure that all parts are present.

    • Cut all solid wood stiles and headers required.

    • Order all required Prefinished tape.

  • Keep the edge tape rack organized.

  • Order necessary items. Edgetape, Glue etc.
