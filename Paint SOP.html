Info:

Whites and Primer shelf life = year-year and 1/2

Coverage:

36 sq. ft. per liter

Application steps for Paint:
  1. Apply the paint by spraying.
  2. Let dry.
  3. Sand (320 grit).
  4. Apply second coat.
  5. Let dry.
Black Paint

Matador Line Black and add:

  • 12% catalyst 309
  • 30% thinner 219

eg. With 1000 ml (1litre) of Matador, add 120 ml of catalyst and 300 ml of thinner.

Frosty White and Antique White paint

Bernyl Strong 20/40 and add:
  • 16% catalyst 309
  • 25% thinner 219

eg. With 1000 ml (1litre) of Bernyl Strong, add 160 ml of catalyst and 250 ml of thinner.

Surfacer White
Add:
  • 12% catalyst 309
  • 25% thinner 219

eg. With 1000 ml (1litre), add 120 ml of catalyst and 250 ml of thinner.

With 20000 ml (20 litre), add 2400 ml of catalyst and 5000 ml of thinner.

Custom Paints
Matador 20/40 and add:
  • 12% catalyst 309
  • 25% thinner 219

eg. With 1000 ml (1litre) of Matador, add 120 ml of catalyst and 250 ml of thinner.
