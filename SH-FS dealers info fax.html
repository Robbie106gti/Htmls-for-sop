Hardware for floating shelf, a.k.a. hidden support or invisible support.

New system:

Why: We need something stronger and more reliable.

Guidelines:

Preset shelf lengths and drilling.
The length options are from 10” to 96”.
The depth options are a minimum of 8” to a maximum of 10”. A depth of 12” is acceptable if one of the ends is fixed to a cabinet. Note that we don’t prep the ends for supports.
Maximum weight of 5 kg./11.02 lb. per foot.
Halogen lights are optional.
The shelf will have as much removed from the interior to keep it light.

Parts:
Rail

Supply an exact length of the dado that was cut for this rail, minus ¼”.

Supports.

Supplier: Nickels (Bob). Material from Complete Metal.
Note that the screw holes are angled at 1 degree to allow for flex.
We will use a minimum of 2 supports per shelf and add a support for every 16” after the initial 16”.

Wall Anchor

Hollow Wall Anchors 3/16S 3/8 drill

We will supply 1 Wall Anchor per support, in a bag.

Screws to attach the support to the rail.

M6 X 16mm with Flange
We will need 2 per support. Attach the supports to the rails in our shop and tape the rails in position to the shelf.

Screws to attach the rail to the studs.

Powerhead 10 x 2.5”
We will supply 1 of these screws per support, in a bag.

The shelf.

Note the dowels to line up the shelves for assembling.
