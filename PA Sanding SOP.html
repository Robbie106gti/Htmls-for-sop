Standards for all items from PA that will be sanded by the Loewer sander :

  • These items are to be sanded with orbital sander (Grit = 120) by hand to remove dents, pencil marks etc. prior to going through the Loewer sander.

Standards for all items from PA that will not be sanded by the Loewer sander :

  • All items that to be sanded correctly and completely with orbital sander (Grit = 120) by hand to remove all flaws prior to going to the Finishing Team.

  • These items will need to be properly marked for finishing.
