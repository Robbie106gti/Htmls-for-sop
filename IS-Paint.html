Dark Paint contents:

We supply Black paint, 5% catalyst and 10% thinner

eg. With 1000 ml paint, add 50 ml of catalyst and 100 ml of thinner.

Mixing instructions:

路 Note that the recommended pot life after mixing is 8 hours and the mixed paint should be used before that time has expired.

路 Mix the supplied catalyst and thinner with the paint before painting.

  • Mix for a minimum of 2 minutes.
White paint contents:

We supply paint, 10% catalyst, 40% thinner.

eg. With 1000 ml (1litre) of paint, add 100 ml of catalyst and 400 ml of thinner.

Mixing instructions:

路 Note that the recommended pot life after mixing is 8 hours and the mixed paint should be used before that time has expired.

路 Mix the supplied catalyst and thinner with the paint before painting.

  • Mix for a minimum of 2 minutes.
Application steps for Paint:
  1. Apply the paint by spraying.
  2. Let dry.
  3. Sand (320 grit).
  4. Apply second coat.
  5. Let dry.
