Mixing Instructions – Touch Up Bottle / Litre / Quart (Clear Coat – Ultraguard)
  1. Before adding Catalyst, shake the finish well (lacquer), vigorously for 3 minutes.
  2. To the original volume of finish (conversion varnish / lacquer), add 10% of Catalyst.
  3. Mix well for one minute.

e.g. For 1000 ml. (1 Litre) of Clear Coat, add 100 ml. of catalyst.
