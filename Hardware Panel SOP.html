Description:

A 5/8” thick panel that matches the cabinet interior and guarantees that the hardware has an appropriate surface to attach to. The hardware panel is attached to the back of the door using the DFS system.

Standards:

  • Made by the Door team.

  • The door will be drilled for the DFS system before finishing.

  • The hardware panel will be drilled for the DFS system before finishing.

  • The face of the hardware panel that is machined for the DFS system need not be finished.

  • The size of the hardware panel is the door height and width minus 41mm (1 5/8”).

  • The face will be predrilled with pilot holes that correspond to the hardware being attached.

  • The hardware panel will be attached to the door and hardware (if applicable) by the DP team.

  • A hardware panel is required for cabinets that have a:

    • DRM-SD

    • BPOD-SR-12CH

    • BPOD-SR-16CH

    • BPOD-SR-18CH

    • BPOD-SR-21CH

    • BPOD-NR-TR

    • BPOD-NR-CW

    • BPOD-NR-2S

    • BPOD-NR-3S

    • BPOD-OLSR’s

    • BPOD-ORSR’s

    • RGB-POD12

    • RGB-POD15

    • RGB-POD18

    • RGB-POD21

    • RGB-POD2B24

    • RGB-POD4B24

    • WPOD-CR-15CH

    • WPOD-CR-18CH

    • TPOD-SR-12CH

    • TPOD-SR-16CH

    • TPOD-SR-18CH

    • TPOD-SR-21CH

Doors > 60” (1524mm) high receive DFS drilling at the center of the door height also.

The face of the hardware panel that is machine for the DFS system need not be finished.
