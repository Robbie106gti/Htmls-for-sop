MAS 90 Data Entry SOP

**Don’t enter orders from the Nickels **

For those of you who use this please note that as of RIGHT NOW please use S for shipping charges to USA as opposed to the SHIP we were using.

Also Canadian Dealers are to have their shipping charges entered from now on & the code for that is SCAN.

  • User ID: SO
  • Password: entry
  • Path: Modules / Sales Order / Main / Sales Order Entry
  • Confirm date on the dialogue box by clicking “OK”.
Items to Fill in
(Page 1) Header
  • Order #: Job #
  • hit enter
  • Customer#: Click on magnifying glass & scroll to the dealer you want.
  • double click
  • If the credit limit has been exceeded, it will warn you here. Email Ted and Accounting how much they are over their limit.
  • Cust. PO #: -Enter customer PO #.
  • Ship Date: -Enter an estimated ship date.
  • Ship To: -Enter job #. (You won’t be able to have a B or C etc. at the end, just the # and this is OK).
  • If “New Ship to address” dialogue box comes up click on yes and select “OK”.
(Page 2) Address

Skip this page.

(Page 3) Lines
  • Item / Kit #: Enter “US” for US jobs / “Cab” or “CPU” for Canadian jobs.
  • hit enter twice
  • Extension: Enter the total dollar amount minus ((currency adj. x shipping) + shipping).
  • hit enter button twice
  • Shipping cost?:
  • If no, go to Page 4.
  • If yes:
  • Item / Kit #: Enter “ship”
  • Extension: Enter the ((currency adj. x shipping) + shipping). Hit enter
(Page 4) Totals
  • Hit accept.
  • Close Program

**Highlight the grand total on the order coversheet to indicate that you have entered the order into MAS90.
