Standards before every assembly session:

Before the process begins, 1 person ensure that the:

  1. “Cut table saw” is cleaned out the first Monday of every month.

  2. Temp. = 18 deg. min.

  3. Dovetail joints are perfect.

  4. Clamp is pressurized properly.

  5. Edgesander is adjusted and ready to accept the full batch of drawer boxes.

  6. “Cut table saw”, notcher and drilling machine are setup and have sharp blades.

  7. Work stations have full Glue bottles, full staplers, enough putty and sandpaper etc.

  8. 1 box ready by the sanding station

  9. 1 box ready in the clamp

  10. 1 box ready by the Assembly station,

  11. 3 boxes ready by the dovetail station.

Lead hand (Position 4) responsibilities:

  1. Have all workers verifiy that their tools are adjusted properly.

  2. Monitor quality of product and stop the processif there is a systematic problem.

Quality checklist:

  • Joints are sound… enough glue was used.

  • Edge sander isn’t sanding through the veneer.

  • All cracks and holes bigger than .5 mm are filled and sanded.

  • All cross grain sanding lines on the sides are gone.
