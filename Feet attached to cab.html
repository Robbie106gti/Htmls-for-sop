1. This setup is used when there are no valances incorporated with the feet.
    Leg/Foot with Pin Lock Carriage bolt

    This is to replace the Foot with a Lag Bolt setup.

    • The feet will be installed ¼” from the outside edges of the cabinet.
    • The bottom shelf of the cabinet must be 5/8” thick plywood and needs 10mm diameter holes drilled for the appropriate amount of carriage bolts and centered over the center of the foot/leg.
    • A bolt shelf is still needed if it isn’t a cabinet with a bank of drawers. The bolt shelf should be finished in the same material as the interior of the cabinet and is routed for the head of the Carriage bolts 9mm deep x 30mm diameter..
    • The foot needs to have a hole (7/8” deep x 14mm diameter) drilled and tapped in the top center.
    • Install a double threaded insert (M10 inside x M14 outside x ¾” long) into the hole in the top of the foot using the cool turning key.
    • Attach the foot to the cabinet using a Pin Lock carriage bolt, 1 ½” long.
    • When assembled in shop, OE must attach a note “” to the cabinet.
    • When assembled on site, add an individual note to “supply all hardware for feet”.
    • The price for this setup is $38.75 per foot whether it is assembled in shop or on site.
